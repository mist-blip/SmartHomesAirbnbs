Our Story

Founded in Nairobi, Smarthomes was born from a passion to showcase Kenya's incredible diversity. From the bustling streets of the capital to the serene beaches of the coast, from safari lodges in the Mara to mountain retreats near Mount Kenya - we connect travelers with unforgettable Kenyan experiences.

We believe every stay should tell a story, and every guest should feel the warmth of Kenyan hospitality. Karibu nyumbani - welcome home! 🇰🇪
